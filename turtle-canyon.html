Turtle Canyon

Shallow reef dives with reliable turtle encounters.

A protected Oahu reef canyon two miles off Waikiki where green sea turtles rest on the plateau, and warm water makes the site easy to enjoy for divers and advanced snorkelers.

Site Overview

Turtle Canyon, Oahu

A layered coral plateau with sandy swim-throughs, broad reef flats, and one of the island’s most dependable green sea turtle populations. The depth and clarity make it a firm favorite for repeat dives.

The canyon is a gradual reef dropoff rather than a wall dive. The dive follows terraces, channels, and small caverns, which means navigational landmarks are easy to read and the pace stays gentle.

What you will see

Green sea turtles feeding and resting on the shelf.
Dense coral gardens of cauliflower coral and blue tangs.
Surgeonfish walls and scattered reef caverns.
Low-angle sunlight through clear shallow water.

Why this charter

Turtle Canyon is a high-return site for small groups, with a short boat ride and predictable conditions. It is especially well suited to travelers who want a relaxed reef dive and a strong chance of seeing honu without deep sea currents.

Depth Range
25–45 ft
Visibility

Recommended experience
Open-water certified divers and advanced snorkelers
