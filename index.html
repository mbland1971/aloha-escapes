Kauai's Na Pali Coast reads nothing like Maui's Road to Hana. The Big Island's volcanic moonscape is a different planet from Oahu's north-shore reef breaks. Each island rewards a different traveler, a different pace, a different kind of attention.

Aloha Escapes was built on a single conviction: the right trip requires knowing which island suits you — then getting you into the parts of it most visitors never find.

We handle every detail: the villa with the private reef, the pre-dawn helicopter above Haleakala, the marine biologist on the whale-watch boat. Your only job is to be present.

What You'll Do There

Private, guided, or independent — every experience below is bookable through us in the format that suits you.

Na Pali Sea Kayak

Sea caves and hidden waterfalls along 17 miles of coast with no road access. Full-day private guide included.

Doors-Off Helicopter

Private flight over Waimea Canyon and Na Pali cliffs. Pre-dawn slots available for the emptiest skies.

Road to Hana Private

64 miles at your pace with a local naturalist who knows which waterfalls stay empty until 9am.

Volcano After Dark

Dusk-to-night walk along active lava fields, guided by a volcanologist. Milky Way above, molten earth below.

Sunrise at Haleakala

Maui's dormant volcano at 10,023 feet. We handle the 3am permit, the shuttle, and the hot coffee.

Humpback Whale Season

December through April, 10,000 humpback whales migrate to Maui's warm waters. We book the research vessel and the marine biologist.

How we work

Three conversations, one custom itinerary, zero generic resort stays. Every trip begins the same way: with your story, not our catalog.

1

The Island Conversation

A 30-minute call with your Aloha Escapes specialist. We ask where you've been, what moved you, what you're looking for — and which island matches your pace. We'll tell you honestly if you're asking for the wrong one.

2

Your Itinerary, Built to Order

Within five business days, we deliver a day-by-day proposal: villa, inter-island connections, activity slots, dining reservations, and a pre-dawn breakfast before the helicopter. Everything adjustable. Nothing locked until you say so.

3

On the Ground, We're Still Here

A local contact number, not a call center. Our team lives on the islands. If a trail washes out or a boat reschedules, we already know — and we've already rebooked you before you've noticed.
